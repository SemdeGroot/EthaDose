# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Hospital pharmacists, clinical toxicology staff, emergency clinicians, and intensive care clinicians who need a local calculation aid during methanol or ethylene glycol intoxication workflows.

## Product Purpose

EthaDose provides a clear local calculation aid for ethanol dosing, based on the provided spreadsheet and source article. It supports calculation and source transparency without replacing local protocols or clinical judgment.

## Brand Personality

Calm, clinical, trustworthy.

## Anti-references

Marketing landing pages, flashy gradients, dark dashboards, gamified medical UI, AI-looking card grids, and interfaces that hide units or make calculations feel opaque.

## Design Principles

- Keep the calculator as the first screen.
- Make units and assumptions visible near results.
- Prefer familiar clinical form controls over decorative interface patterns.
- Separate source context from the calculation itself.
- Keep disclaimers present but visually quiet.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Use high-contrast text, touch-friendly form controls, visible focus states on web, field-level errors, and reduced cognitive load.
